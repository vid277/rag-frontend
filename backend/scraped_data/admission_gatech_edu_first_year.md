## First-Year Admission

As you begin your search for the right fit for your college experience, we’re thrilled that you’re considering Georgia Tech! As you embark on this journey, we want you to know our team is here to help guide you through the admission process and help you learn everything you need to know about Georgia Tech. Here are some resources and information to help you get started, including admission application deadlines, requirements, information about the review process, and more.

### Ready to Apply?

Georgia Tech exclusively uses the [Common App](https://www.commonapp.org/) for first-year admission. You will need to create a Common App account before starting your application, if you do not already have one.

[![A button that reads ‘start your application’.](https://admission.gatech.edu/images/StartYourApplication-button.jpg)](https://www.commonapp.org/explore/georgia-institute-technology)
