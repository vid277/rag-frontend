## Financial Aid and Scholarships FAQs

1. Can financial aid be used on a study abroad program?
   - Yes. When Georgia Tech students study abroad on a Georgia Tech program, their financial aid travels with them - including scholarships, grants and loans.
2. Are there Scholarships for Study Abroad?
   - Yes. There are scholarships available. Please visit [http://oie.gatech.edu/find\_scholarships](http://oie.gatech.edu/find_scholarships) to search our database. If there are questions, don’t hesitate to reach out to [education.abroad](mailto:education.abroad@oie.gatech.edu) [@oie.gatech.ed](mailto:scholarships@oie.gatech.edu) u
3. Looking for more information? The Education Abroad Office offers sessions throughout the semester with information about financial resources and studying abroad. Please visit our [schedule of events](https://oie.gatech.edu/ "https://oie.gatech.edu/") to see any upcoming sessions. If you have any questions, please email [education.abroad@oie.gatech.edu](mailto:education.abroad@oie.gatech.edu "mailto:education.abroad@oie.gatech.edu")
